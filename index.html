About

Santiago Peña De Jesús was born in 2011 in San Juan, Puerto Rico. He began playing the violin at the age of three, learning through the Suzuki Method. Since then, he has continued his musical journey with a mix of private lessons, group classes, workshops, and master classes, studying under a variety of teachers who have shaped his development as a violinist. Santiago is currently a student at the Escuela Libre de Música Ernesto Ramos Antonini, where he pursues both academic and intensive musical studies. Over the years, he has gained experience performing solo and with ensembles, building a strong foundation in both technique and musical expression. Now 14 years old, Santiago remains passionate about the violin and continues to grow as a performer and musician, always seeking new opportunities to learn and share his music.
